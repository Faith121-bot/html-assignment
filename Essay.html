DEFINITION OF ART

Art is a divese range of human activity and resulting product, that involves creative or imaginative talent expressive of proficiency, beauty, emotional power, or conceptual ideas.

Art is also something that is created with imagination and skill that is beautiful or that expresses important ideas or feelings

Some basic elements of art are;Colour, Line, Shape, Form, and Texture.

TYPES OF ART

  • Painting: It is the act or process of using paint. The paint can be used more practically as a protective coating form of decoration. Painting are a form of visual art that captures the expression of ideas and emotions on a two-dimensional surface.
  • Sculptures: Is an artistic form in which hard or plastiic materials are worked into three-dimensional art objects. The desings may be embodied in freestanding objects, in reliefs on surfaces, or in environments ranging from tableaux to contexts that envelop the spectator.
